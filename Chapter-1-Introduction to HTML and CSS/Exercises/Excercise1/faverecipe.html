London Fish & Chips

Fish and chips is a classic dish consisting of fried fish in batter, served with chips. The dish was originated in England.

Ingredients Used

  • 1kg of white fish fillets
  • 1kg of potatoes
  • 1kg of all purpose flour
  • salt, to taste
  • 1/3 Cup Sparkling water
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable Oil

Instruction

  1. Lay fish fillets on a paper towel and pat dry. Season lightly with a little salt.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sparkling water, freshly ground pepper & coat fish.
  3. Heat the Vegetable oil to 350 F in a deep fryer.
  4. Lower each fillet in the hot oil gradually.
  5. Fry until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Now, Heat the oil to 400 F, then cook the chips in batches until golden and crisp.
  8. Serve the yummylicious dish immediately enjoy it with your choice of drink.
