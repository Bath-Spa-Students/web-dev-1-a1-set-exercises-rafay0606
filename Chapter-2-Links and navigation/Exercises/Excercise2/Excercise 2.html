Plot

Titanic tells the tragic love story of Jack Dawson and Rose DeWitt Bukater, set against the backdrop of the ill-fated maiden voyage of the Cruise Titanic

Key Information

The Titanic, which was launched on May 31, 1911, embarked on its inaugural journey from Southampton on April 10, 1912, carrying 2,240 passengers and crew. Tragically, on April 15, 1912, the Titanic collided with an iceberg, leading to its disintegration and descent to the ocean floor, resulting in the loss of over 1,500 lives among the passengers and crew.

Characters

Jack Dawson - Played by Leonardo DiCaprio, Jack is a young, free-spirited artist who wins a third-class ticket aboard the Titanic in a poker game. He represents the vitality and optimism of the lower class passengers.

Rose DeWitt Bukater - Played by Kate Winslet, Rose is a young woman from a wealthy family who is engaged to marry Cal Hockley. However, she feels trapped in her privileged but suffocating life. Rose meets Jack aboard the Titanic and finds herself drawn to his carefree spirit and zest for life.

Cal Hockley - Played by Billy Zane, Cal is Rose's wealthy and controlling fianc√©. He embodies the societal expectations and constraints of the upper class during the early 20th century. Cal is possessive of Rose and is determined to maintain his social status at any cost.

Rose's Mother (Ruth DeWitt Bukater) - Played by Frances Fisher, Ruth is Rose's mother who is primarily concerned with preserving her family's social standing. She pressures Rose to marry Cal for financial security and is dismissive of Rose's desires and feelings.

Captain Edward James Smith - Played by Bernard Hill, Captain Smith is the experienced and respected captain of the Titanic. He is depicted as a capable leader who ultimately faces the tragic consequences of the ship's collision with an iceberg.

Thomas Andrews - Played by Victor Garber, Andrews is the chief designer of the Titanic. He is portrayed as deeply troubled by the ship's sinking and its failure to have enough lifeboats to accommodate all the passengers.
